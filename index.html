About IEEE

The IEEE Student Branch at RGPV serves as a dynamic platform for students passionate about technology, innovation, and professional growth. Our branch strives to create an environment where students can explore emerging technologies, develop technical expertise, and cultivate leadership and teamwork skills through a variety of activities.
We organize workshops, seminars, technical talks, hackathons, and competitions to provide hands-on learning experiences and to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and practical application. By encouraging collaboration and knowledge-sharing, we aim to inspire students to think creatively, solve real-world problems, and contribute to the advancement of technology.
Beyond technical development, our branch also focuses on nurturing professional and ethical values. We encourage participation in community outreach programs, technical projects, and networking opportunities that help students build confidence, communication skills, and a sense of responsibility toward society.
Through these initiatives, the IEEE Student Branch at RGPV seeks to empower students to become innovative thinkers, effective leaders, and responsible technologists who are ready to make meaningful contributions to the world of engineering and technology.

Who We Are

The IEEE RGPV Student Branch is a community of students passionate about technology and its potential to transform society. We are part of the world's largest technical professional organization dedicated to advancing technology for the benefit of humanity.

Our branch organizes technical workshops, seminars, hackathons, and various events that provide students with opportunities to learn, network, and develop their skills in various domains of engineering and technology.
